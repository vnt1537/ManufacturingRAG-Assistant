# Product Specification Sheet
Document ID: PS-MH-204
Component: Servo Motor Housing
Part Number: MH-204-A
Revision: B
Effective Date: 2026-02-14
Department: Mechanical Engineering
Approved By: A. Schneider

## 1. Scope
This specification defines the material, dimensional, surface, and assembly-related requirements for the servo motor housing used in Model XR-7 assembly systems.

## 2. Material
Base material shall be Aluminium Alloy EN AW-6082 T6.
Material certificate according to EN 10204 3.1 is required for each production batch.

## 3. Critical Dimensions
Outer diameter: 120.0 ± 0.1 mm  
Inner bore diameter: 85.0 ± 0.05 mm  
Overall height: 64.0 ± 0.1 mm  
Mounting face flatness: max. 0.08 mm  
Bolt hole diameter: 6.6 ± 0.05 mm

## 4. Surface Requirements
Surface roughness on sealing face shall not exceed Ra 1.6 µm.
No visible cracks, dents, or corrosion marks are permitted.
Cosmetic scratches on non-functional outer surfaces shall not exceed 8 mm in length or 0.2 mm in width.

## 5. Threaded Fasteners
Housing cover shall be fixed using 4 × M6 stainless steel screws.
Required tightening torque: 9.5 to 10.5 Nm.
Torque shall be verified once per batch start and then every 2 hours during production.

## 6. Temperature and Environmental Limits
Operating temperature range: -10°C to 65°C.
Storage temperature range: -20°C to 75°C.
Relative humidity during storage shall not exceed 85 percent non-condensing.

## 7. Inspection Requirements
First article inspection is mandatory for the first 5 parts of each new batch.
Dimensional inspection frequency after release: every 20th part.
Torque verification records shall be maintained in the batch log.

## 8. Rejection Criteria
Any housing with mounting face flatness above 0.08 mm shall be rejected.
Any part with burr height above 0.05 mm on sealing edges shall be rejected.
Any thread damage affecting screw engagement shall be classified as critical defect.
